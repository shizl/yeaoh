-- SUMMARY --

Provides a fieldset formatter for the Field Collection module.
After enabling the module, go to
admin/structure/types/manage/CONTENT_TYPE/display
and switch the formatter to "Fieldset of field collection items".

-- REQUIREMENTS --

http://drupal.org/project/field_collection
